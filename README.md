# CedarwoodOS

A universal business operations terminal. Ask questions, manage knowledge, upload documents, track tickets, and communicate with your team — all from one interface.

Built for contractors and businesses that need instant access to their operational knowledge without calling the owner or digging through binders.

## What It Does

- **Terminal:** Ask any question about your business. AI answers from your own data.
- **+ Update:** Add knowledge to the system. Type it in or upload a document.
- **Document Upload:** Drop PDFs, Word docs, spec sheets. The system extracts and stores.
- **Tickets:** Track issues, tasks, and follow-ups.
- **Messages:** Slack integration for team communication with AI assistance.
- **Quick Links:** One-click access to your most-used tools and portals.

## Stack

- Frontend: Next.js (Vercel)
- Backend: Express/TypeScript (Railway)
- Database: PostgreSQL (Railway)
- AI: Claude API (Anthropic)
- Messaging: Slack

## Origin

Forked from ClubOS, a production facility management system running 14 golf simulator bays across 5 locations with zero employees. CedarwoodOS extracts the universal operations layer and makes it deployable for any business.
